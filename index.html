Southampton Interunis 2025 at Aston Down

From the 26th of July to the 3rd of August, Southampton is excited to be hosting the inter-university gliding competition for 2025 at Aston Down with the help of the Cotswold Gliding Club!

We are looking forward to welcoming you all to the event, and we hope you are as excited as we are! We are currently working on the website, so please check back soon for more information.
